# Add Your Photos & Videos Here

Copy your files into this `assets` folder and rename them exactly as shown:

| Your file | Rename to | Used for |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Your first birthday photo | `photo1.jpg` | Gift 1 (photo + message 1) |
| `IMG_7623.MOV.mp4` (or your video) | `video1.mp4` | Gift 2 (video + message 2) |
| Your third birthday photo | `photo2.jpg` | Gift 3 (photo + message 3) |
| Optional video thumbnail | `video-poster.jpg` | Shown before video plays |

## Notes

- **Photos:** JPG or PNG both work. If your photo is `.png`, either rename to `photo1.png` and update `index.html`, or convert to JPG.
- **Video:** MP4 works best on phones and laptops. Your `IMG_7623.MOV.mp4` file should be copied here as `video1.mp4`.
- If `1000166785.mp4` is your video instead, copy that one as `video1.mp4`.
